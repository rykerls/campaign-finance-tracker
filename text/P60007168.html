About 80% of contributions made by individuals to Bernie Sander's campaign have come from donations under $200 dollars. This makes Bernie's campaign the highest in receiving contributions under $200. This amount of contributions under $200 is quite remarkable because the total amount of receipts received for his campaign is about $96 million. In addition, about 17.85% of donations were given by individuals between $200 to $1,500 and only 1.63% was given above $1,500.

Bernie has received the most money for his 2016 campaign from the state of Massachusetts at about $12 million. The state of California is in second contributing about $3 million and the state of New York is in third giving a total of about $1.2 million in donations.
